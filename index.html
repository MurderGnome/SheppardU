About Sheppard University

Your partner in professional growth and lifelong learning

Sheppard University champions the professional and personal development of Airmen and their families at Sheppard AFB. We recognize and reward your commitment to education through college courses, professional certifications, and on-base training programs. By partnering with leading organizations across the installation, we deliver diverse learning opportunities designed to build resilience, boost confidence, and strengthen our community. Your success is our mission.

Path to Graduation

Achieve your goals and earn recognition for your dedication

Graduate in 6 months by earning 50 points. Ceremonies align with CCAF graduation in April and October.

Point Values

Activity Points Earned
CLEP/DANTES Exam 4 points
Undergraduate Credit Hour 3 points per credit
Graduate Credit Hour 4 points per credit
Sheppard University Course 4 points per course

Achievement Tiers

Tier Requirements Recognition
Graduate 50 points Graduation Certificate
Wingman 75 points + Facilitate 1 Course Certificate + Commemorative Coin
Leader 100 points + Facilitate 2 Courses Certificate + Coin + Wall of Fame

Getting Started

Your journey to professional growth starts here

Ready to Start? Follow these steps to enroll and track your progress TODAY!

1 Enroll in Sheppard University
  • Download the Class Participation Document
  • Check the latest newsletter for available classes.
  • Bring your Class Participation Document with you.
  • Have the instructor sign your Class Participation Document.
2 Report Classes
  • Self-report all classes taken via the Microsoft Form.
3 Upload Signed Document
  • Upload your signed Class Participation Document before 01 March (April Graduation) or before 01 September (October Graduation).

Drag and drop your file here or click to browse

4 Validation
  • Sheppard University Council members will verify your uploaded Class Participation Document against your self-reported classes to confirm your points.
